CNC Turning
Introduction

Computer Numerical Control(CNC) is a self-contained numerical control(NC) system for a single machine tool including a dedicated minicomputer controlled by stored instructions to perform some or all of the basic NC functions. It is the automated control of machining tools such as drills, lathes and mills. CNC machine is the last step in the metal manufacturing, sometimes it will be the only process involved.
When compared to the other working techniques, CNC is capable of satisfiying close tolerances and gives excellent surface finish.
